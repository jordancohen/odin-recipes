Lasagna

Description

This recipe makes 12 servings, each containing 25g of protein and 200 calories.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of lean ground beef
  • 6 oz. whole wheat lasanga noodles
  • 2.5 cups Hunts traditional spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups fat free cottage cheese
  • 9 oz. pkg. frozen spinach
  • 8 egg whites
  • 8 oz. mushrooms
  • 1 cup fat free mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until brown.
  2. Boil noodles as directed by package.
  3. Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray and spread a few tablespoons of the spaghetti sauce on the bottom of the pan.
  4. Mix the rest of the ingredients, except for the fat free mozzarella cheese.
  5. Lay down half of the noodles and top with half of the cottage cheese mixture.
  6. Layer with the rest of the noodles and top with the other half of the mixture.
  7. Lastly, put the fat free cheese on top.
  8. Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
